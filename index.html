Questions

Before you request an invite

Is CoachOS live right now?

Not to the public. We're in invite-only early access, onboarding a small first cohort of real coaches before opening up more broadly. Requesting an invite gets you in line for the next cohort.

Why invite-only instead of open signup?

A platform this broad — leads, delivery, contracts, payments, all AI-assisted — is worth getting right with a small group first. Invite-only lets us onboard each coach properly and fold real feedback into the product before scaling access.

What does "AI drafts, you approve" actually mean?

AI writes the first draft of summaries, follow-ups, and content across the platform — but nothing sends, posts, or charges a client without you reviewing and approving the exact wording first. No exceptions, anywhere in the product.

How much will it cost?

Pricing is being finalized for the early access cohort. Coaches who join now lock in founding-member pricing when it's confirmed — you won't pay more later for joining early.

Is my client data safe?

Coaching notes are sensitive by nature, and we treat security as non-negotiable — client data stays private to you, is never used to train any model, and an independent security review happens before real client data flows through the platform.
