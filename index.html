Questions

Before you request an invite

Is CoachOS live right now?

Not to the public. We're in invite-only early access, onboarding a small first cohort of real coaches, mentors, and freelancers before opening up more broadly. Requesting an invite gets you in line for the next cohort.

Why invite-only instead of open signup?

A platform this broad — leads, delivery, contracts, marketing, payments, all AI-assisted — is worth getting right with a small group first. Invite-only lets us onboard each business properly and fold real feedback into the product before scaling access.

What's actually done by a human vs. AI?

Every capability on this page is tagged plainly — Human, AI, or Human + AI. Some things (your website build, domain setup, the community) are genuinely done by people. Most day-to-day execution — drafting, chasing, flagging, following up — is AI, reviewed and approved by you.

How much will it cost?

Pricing is being finalized for the early access cohort. Coaches, mentors, and freelancers who join now lock in founding-member pricing when it's confirmed — you won't pay more later for joining early.

Is my client data safe?

Client and coaching notes are sensitive by nature, and we treat security as non-negotiable — your data stays private to you, is never used to train any model, and an independent security review happens before real client data flows through the platform.
