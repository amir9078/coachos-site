The Solo Business AI-Agent Starter Checklist.

Twelve concrete steps for setting up your first AI-assisted follow-up, invoice reminder, and content workflow — usable with or without CoachOS. Print it, or save this page.

Leads & follow-up

✓

1. Write down your actual second-follow-up rule

Decide, in writing, how many days after silence you follow up, and with what tone — most lost leads are lost to "I meant to," not a bad pitch.

✓

2. Keep one place for every enquiry, not your inbox

A spreadsheet, a simple board, anything — as long as "where did this lead come from and what happened" is answerable in ten seconds.

✓

3. Note what actually worked, per lead type

Direct pitch vs. soft check-in, referral vs. cold — a short note next to each converted lead becomes your own playbook over time.

Client delivery

✓

4. Record or note every session, even roughly

Messy notes are fine — the point is having something to turn into a summary, rather than reconstructing it from memory a week later.

✓

5. Send a written summary within 24 hours

Clients remember far less than they think. A same-day summary is one of the highest-leverage habits in this whole list.

✓

6. Track goals somewhere the client can also see

Even a shared doc beats "it's in my head" — visible progress is what keeps a client engaged between sessions.

Billing

✓

7. Set a fixed invoice-reminder schedule

Day 3, day 7, day 14 — pick numbers and stick to them, so chasing payment stops being an awkward judgment call every time.

✓

8. Track package/credit usage, not just totals

Know when a client is at 80% of a package before it runs out — that's your renewal-conversation window, not after.

Marketing & content

✓

9. Keep a running list of your real themes

The questions clients actually ask you are your best content ideas — write them down as they come up, don't try to invent topics from scratch.

✓

10. Set one realistic sending cadence and hold it

Monthly and consistent beats weekly and abandoned after six weeks — pick the cadence you'll actually keep.

The agentic layer

✓

11. Decide what you'll never let an AI send unreviewed

Draft anything you like with AI — but decide up front what always gets a human read before it goes out. Write that line down.
